Our Journey

The Cozy Bean Cafe started as a small dream in a home kitchen. Our founder, Grishma Neupane, spent years perfecting the art of coffee roasting, sharing her creations with friends and family.

Driven by a passion for community and quality, she opened our doors to the public. Our vision is simple: to be the heart of the neighborhood.
